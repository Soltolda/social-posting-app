Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This application ("Soltolda Properties") uses the Meta (Facebook & Instagram) API to allow users to publish content to their social media accounts.

We do not store, sell, or share user data with any third parties.

Data We Access

  • Public account ID
  • API permission tokens
  • Content provided for posting (captions, images, videos)

This information is used only for user-requested actions such as posting content to Instagram or Facebook.

We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identifying information
  • Passwords
  • Private messages
  • Financial or sensitive data

Revoking Permissions

You may revoke permissions anytime through your Meta account settings:

https://www.facebook.com/settings?tab=applications
